# 🚗 Pasahero App - A Fun Guide for Everyone!

## Welcome to Pasahero! 👋

Imagine you have a magical app that helps you get rides from one place to another, just like calling a friend to pick you up! That's what **Pasahero** does. It's like a game where you can be a **Passenger** (the person getting a ride) or a **Driver** (the person giving rides).

---

## 🎮 The Two Main Roles

### 👤 **Passenger** - You're the Rider!
You want to go somewhere, so you use the app to find a driver.

### 🚙 **Driver** - You're the Helper!
You have a vehicle and you help passengers get to where they need to go. You earn money! 💰

---

## 🏠 **Passenger Features** - How to Get a Ride

### 1. **Welcome Screen** 🎬
When you first open the app, you see a cool welcome page that asks: "Are you a Passenger or a Driver?"
- Pick **Passenger** if you need a ride
- Pick **Driver** if you want to give rides

### 2. **Login & Register** 🔐
Just like logging into a game:
- **Login**: If you already have an account, enter your phone number and password
- **Register**: If you're new, create an account with your name, phone number, and password

### 3. **Home Screen** 🏠
This is like your main dashboard. You see:
- **Your Wallet Balance** 💵 - How much money you have in your Pasahero wallet
- **Ride Type Options** 🏍️ - Choose what kind of vehicle you want:
  - **Habal-habal** - A motorcycle (fast and cheap!)
  - **Bao-bao** - A bigger vehicle (more comfortable!)
- **Quick Action Buttons** - Shortcuts to book a ride, check history, or manage your wallet
- **Saved Locations** - Your favorite places like Home 🏠 and Work 💼

### 4. **Search Ride Screen** 🗺️
Now you're ready to find a ride!
- **Pick Your Starting Point** - Where are you right now?
- **Pick Your Destination** - Where do you want to go?
- **See the Estimated Fare** - How much will it cost? 💰
- **Use Saved Locations** - Tap "Home" or "Work" to quickly fill in addresses

### 5. **Driver List Screen** 👨‍🚗
The app shows you all available drivers:
- **Driver Name** - Who's picking you up?
- **Vehicle Type** - What are they driving?
- **Rating** ⭐ - How good are they? (5 stars = awesome!)
- **ETA** - How long until they arrive?
- **Fare** - How much will it cost?
- **View Full Profile** - Click to see more about the driver before booking

### 6. **Ride Tracking Screen** 📍
Your driver is on the way!
- **Live Map** - See exactly where your driver is
- **Driver Info Card** - Name, vehicle, phone number
- **Real-Time ETA** - How many minutes until they arrive?
- **Call or Message** - Talk to your driver if needed
- **Share Location** - Let friends know where you are

### 7. **Ride Ongoing Screen** 🚗💨
The ride has started!
- **Live Map** - Watch the route in real-time
- **Trip Timer** - How long have you been riding?
- **Pickup & Dropoff** - Where you started and where you're going
- **Distance Remaining** - How much further?
- **Share Location** - Keep your family updated

### 8. **Ride Complete Screen** ✅
The ride is done! Now:
- **Rate Your Driver** ⭐ - Give them 1-5 stars
- **Leave a Comment** - Tell them what you thought
- **Add a Tip** 💵 - Give them extra money if they were great
- **Choose Payment Method** - Pay with cash, GCash, or wallet
- **See Receipt** - Get a copy of your trip details

### 9. **Ride History Screen** 📋
See all your past rides:
- **Trip Details** - Where you went and when
- **Fare Amount** - How much you paid
- **Driver Info** - Who drove you
- **Rating You Gave** - Your review
- **Click for Details** - See more info about any trip

### 10. **Wallet Screen** 💳
Your money management center:
- **Current Balance** - How much money you have
- **Cash In Button** - Add money to your wallet
- **Recent Transactions** - Your last few payments
- **Quick Actions** - Pay for rides, check history

### 11. **Cash In Screen** 💰
Add money to your wallet:
- **Enter Amount** - How much do you want to add?
- **Quick Buttons** - Tap ₱50, ₱100, ₱500, etc. for quick selection
- **Payment Method** - Choose:
  - **GCash** - Instant transfer (fastest!)
  - **Maya** - Bank transfer
  - **Manual** - Admin helps you (slower but works)
- **Confirm** - Complete the transaction

### 12. **Wallet History Screen** 📊
See all your money transactions:
- **Filter by Type** - All, Rides, Cash In, Refunds
- **Transaction List** - Each payment or deposit
- **Click for Details** - See full info about any transaction
- **Pull to Refresh** - Swipe down to update

### 13. **Saved Locations Screen** 📍
Your favorite places:
- **Home** 🏠 - Your house address
- **Work** 💼 - Your office address
- **Favorite** ⭐ - Any other place you go often
- **Edit** - Change an address
- **Delete** - Remove a location
- **Quick Book** - Tap to instantly book a ride to that place

### 14. **Notifications Screen** 🔔
Messages from drivers:
- **Driver Messages** - Drivers can send you updates
- **Ride Alerts** - Important ride information
- **Promotions** - Special offers and discounts
- **Mark as Read** - Keep track of what you've seen

### 15. **Profile Screen** 👤
Your account information:
- **Your Name** - What people call you
- **Phone Number** - How to reach you
- **Email** - Your email address
- **Payment Methods** - Your saved cards and wallets
- **Settings** - Change app preferences
- **Logout** - Sign out of the app

## 🎯 **How a Ride Works** - Step by Step

### **For Passengers:**
1. Open app → Pick "Passenger"
2. Login or Register
3. Go to Home screen
4. Choose ride type (Habal-habal or Bao-bao)
5. Click "Book a Ride"
6. Enter pickup and dropoff locations
7. See estimated fare
8. Browse available drivers
9. Click "Order Ride"
10. Watch driver come to you on the map
11. Driver arrives and picks you up
12. Enjoy the ride!
13. Rate and pay when done
14. See receipt in history

### **For Drivers:**
1. Open app → Pick "Driver"
2. Login or Register
3. Go to Driver Home
4. Click "Go Online"
5. Wait for ride requests
6. Get notification of a passenger request
7. See pickup and dropoff locations
8. Click "Accept" within 30 seconds
9. Navigate to pickup location
10. Pick up passenger
11. Navigate to dropoff location
12. Complete the trip
13. Get paid! 💰
14. See earnings in wallet

## 💡 **Smart Features**

### **Fare Calculation** 💰
The app automatically calculates how much a ride costs based on:
- Distance traveled
- Vehicle type
- Time of day

The app makes sure you don't accidentally book two rides at the same time.

### **30-Second Countdown** ⏱️
Drivers have 30 seconds to accept a ride request. If they don't, the app finds another driver.

### **Real-Time Updates** 🔄
Everything updates instantly - no waiting for old information.

### **Accessibility** ♿
Text sizes adjust automatically so everyone can read it comfortably.

## ❓ **FAQ - Questions You Might Have**

### **Q: Is my location safe?**
A: Yes! Your location is only shared with your driver during the ride, and only for that trip.

### **Q: What if I don't like my driver?**
A: You can rate them honestly, and the app will try to match you with someone else next time.

### **Q: How do I get my money as a driver?**
A: Go to your wallet and click "Withdraw". Choose your payment method and the money goes to your account!

### **Q: Can I cancel a ride?**
A: Yes! You can cancel before the driver arrives. There might be a small fee if you cancel too late.

### **Q: What if there's a problem?**
A: Contact support through the app. They'll help you out!

### **Q: How much does it cost?**
A: It depends on the distance and vehicle type. You'll see the estimated fare before you book.

### **Q: Can I save my favorite drivers?**
A: Not yet, but you can rate them so you remember who was good!
